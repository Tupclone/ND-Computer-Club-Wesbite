ND Computer Club

About

The Notre Dame Computer Club encompasses all things computer-related, ranging from in-depth development topics to practical productivity tools. We organize events designed to help members acquire valuable computer skills, advance their careers in the tech industry, or simply engage in casual discussions about their interests in computer hardware and software.
